Summary

2D 1vs1 RTS in which you grow monsters in your half of the lawn to steal your opponents golden eggs without losing yours.

Project Details

Made as a one-semester class project with a team of 4 programmers and 1 musician. Made with Alpha Engine (very simple c++ game engine without editor, we had to program many features ourselves).
