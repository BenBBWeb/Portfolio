Summary

2D vehicle action-adventure side scroller where you drive your flying vessel through the skies. Use the different stations on your ship to protect it against obstacles and enemies.

Project Details

Made as a one-year class project with a team of 5 artists, 5 programmers and 1 musician. The engine was done by us entirelly with c++ and using OpenGL as the graphics API.
