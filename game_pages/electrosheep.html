Gameplay Video

Summary

Make the sheep transport electricity from the anode to the cathode by luring them with apples. Place other objects to avoid getting them eaten by the wolves.

Project Details

Made with unity in 48h for the Global Game Jam 2018 (Theme: "transmission") by a team of 2. In our game a sheep gets charged when in proximity of the anode or another charged sheep, then it can only lose its charge if it wanders far enough of all other sheeps and the anode. Funnily enough, this last part was a bug but we decided to keep it as needing to have all the sheep form a path from anode to cathode would make for a very difficult game.
