Project Orion

3D spaceship piloting game. Follow the path and destroy the enemy turrets.

Made as a one-year class project with a team of 7 programmers. The engine is entirelly done by us.

SkyWolf

2D vehicle action-adventure side scroller where you drive your flying vessel through the skies. Use the different stations on your ship to protect it against obstacles and enemies.

PokeOff

2D 1vs1 fighting game in which you control everbouncing characters and try to push your opponent out of the pilars. The pilars move down with each bounce! There are 4 playable characters.

Lawn Of The Dead

2D 1vs1 RTS in which you grow monsters in your half of the lawn to steal your opponents golden eggs without losing yours.

ElectroSheep

Quick puzzle game were you place items on the ground to get electric sheep gather charge at the anode and bring it to the cathode. Beware the dangerous foxes!

Made for the Global Game Jam 2018. The theme was 'Transmission'.
